What is architecture?

Today, the word architecture is used in two related meanings:

1- Architecture is a process of organizing space that has been named and pays attention to a creative activity of human beings and arises on the basis of scientific-experimental, art and technology of construction. This perception is mostly done by architects in the category of architecture.

2- Architecture as an achievement of organizing space or an architectural work that is considered the name of essence and to Refers to buildings that have undergone this process before construction. This concept is mostly used by archaeologists and architectural historians.
